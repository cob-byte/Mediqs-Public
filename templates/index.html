Services Offered

EMR System

A web-based electronic medical record (EMR) system to facilitate healthcare management in barangay providing a secure and reliable way of storing and accessing comprehensive patient information.





Digitizing Tasks

There is a digitalization of administrative works such as patient registration, appointment, scheduling, monitoring of medical supplies and equipment, writing a prescription, and billing process.






Appointment System

Efficiently manage patient appointments and schedules in the Barangay Health Center and improve healthcare providers' access to patients' appointment histories and follow-up appointments, reducing wait times and improving patient flow.



Predictive Analytics

Implementation of an outbreak prediction analytics system that utilizes real-time data and machine learning algorithms to accurately predict outbreaks and provide timely insights to healthcare professionals for effective preventive measures.

ABOUT

Mediqs is a web-based platform for multiple healthcare centers within Manila area. This is a platform for the barangay community for easier process in healthcare management.

It provides medical assistance to patients who require healthcare services in the barangay. It has the ability to track, monitor, and inform patients' health records digitally.

Patients can also view information regarding multiple healthcare centers and make appointments online, and doctors can manage the records of their patients.
